Cheesecake

Photo by A Studios on Unsplash

Description

This delectable sweet dish reminds me of my first date. The perfect cheesy, sweet, a little salty flavor. The soft melt-in-your-mouth texture. The contrasting crumbly bits from the cake base. The slight tease of the cranberry sauce. Truly, this inspires and fuels everlasting love.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 packs 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can cherrie pie filling
  • 1 pan with graham pie crust

Steps

  1. Beat the cream cheese for 2-3 minutes
  2. Whisk in the sugar and mix for 1 minute
  3. Add flour and sugar and mix for 1 minute
  4. Put the sour cream and mix for 1 minute
  5. Add the egg one at a time, mix until all homogenous
  6. Pour the cheesecake mixture over the graham crust
  7. Preheat the oven at about 160 centigrade
  8. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes
  9. Place the cherry pie filling on top
  10. Rest and refrigerate
  11. Serve and enjoy
